How to use this tool to analyze P, QRS, and T waves:
  1. CLOSE Arduino IDE Serial Monitor or Plotter. (Only one program can use the COM port at a time).
  2. Click Connect to ESP32 and select your COM port.
  3. Once data is streaming, click Pause for Analysis to freeze the screen.
  4. Zoom In: Click and drag a box over a specific heartbeat on the graph to zoom into the P-QRS-T complex.
  5. Zoom Out: Double-click anywhere on the graph to reset the zoom.
